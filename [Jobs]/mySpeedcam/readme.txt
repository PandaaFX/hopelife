Installation:
1.) Put the scripts in your ressources folder
2.) Start NativeUI before mySpeedcam
4.) Import the speedcam.sql to your database
5.) Add 'start mySpeedcam' to your server.cfg. After ES_Extended!

Pay attention that ESX and NativeUI are running and are started before mySpeedcam.
Correct NativeUI version can be downloaded on my Discord.
NativeUI GitHub: https://github.com/FrazzIe/NativeUILua


Commands:
- /createSpeedcam
- /speedcams
